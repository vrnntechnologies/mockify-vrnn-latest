About Us

We are Mockify AI

Mockify AI is revolutionizing the way job seekers prepare for interviews. Born from the need to make high-quality interview coaching accessible to everyone, we leverage advanced generative AI to simulate realistic interview scenarios.

Our intelligent system adapts to your responses, providing real-time feedback on your speech patterns, technical accuracy, and soft skills.

Our Mission

To democratize career coaching by providing an accessible, intelligent, and personalized interview practice platform that empowers every individual to realize their full professional potential.

Our Vision

A world where anxiety is replaced by confidence, and talent is the only barrier to entry. We envision a future where AI bridges the gap between preparation and opportunity.
